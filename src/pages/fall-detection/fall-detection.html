Fall Detection

Welcome to the Fall Detection page of the app!
The application will monitor your phone to see if it detects a fall!

If a fall is detected an alarm will play giving you 30 seconds to respond to cancel the alarm. However, if you have actually fallen and are unable to respond to the alarm, your mobile phone will text the Mourne Mountain Rescue Team giving them your location! The application will even monitor your phone in the backround so you don't need to keep your app open while hiking!

Please remember to turn your volume up high so you can hear the alarm!
